Chicken Curry Recipe:
Ingredients:
- 1 lb chicken breast, cut into pieces
- 2 tbsp curry powder
- 1 can coconut milk
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onion, garlic, and ginger.
2. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned.
3. Stir in curry powder and cook for another minute.
4. Pour in coconut milk, bring to a boil, then simmer until chicken is cooked through.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice.
