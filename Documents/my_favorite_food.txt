Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe:
Ingredients:
- 200g spaghetti
- 100g pancetta
- 2 large eggs
- 50g pecorino cheese
- 50g parmesan cheese
- Freshly ground black pepper
- Salt

Instructions:
1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
3. Beat the eggs in a bowl, then add grated pecorino and parmesan.
4. Drain the pasta and mix with the pancetta, remove from heat.
5. Quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture, adding pasta water if needed.
6. Serve with extra cheese and black pepper.
